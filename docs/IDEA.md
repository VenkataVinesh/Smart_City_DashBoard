# Smart City IoT Intelligence Dashboard

## Problem Statement
Rapid urbanization has made city management increasingly complex. Decision-makers often lack a unified, real-time view of critical metrics like air quality, traffic congestion, and weather conditions across different regions. This fragmentation leads to delayed responses to environmental hazards and traffic crises.

## Why Smart City Dashboards Matter
A centralized intelligence dashboard bridges the gap between raw data and actionable insights. By aggregating disparate IoT sensor data into a single, intuitive interface, city administrators can:
- **Visualize** the heartbeat of multiple cities simultaneously.
- **Respond** faster to emergencies through automated alert systems.
- **Analyze** long-term trends to inform urban planning and policy.

## Real-world Use Cases
1. **Emergency Services:** Dispatching ambulances based on real-time traffic "quiet zones."
2. **Public Health:** Issuing air quality warnings to vulnerable populations in specific neighborhoods.
3. **Urban Planning:** Identifying traffic bottlenecks for future infrastructure development.
4. **Environmental Monitoring:** Tracking the impact of local policies on city-wide AQI levels.
